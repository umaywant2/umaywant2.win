## 3️⃣ Sign‑Up Page Language (Calm, Legal‑Clean, College‑Style)

This reads like a university challenge or lab sprint — neutral, safe, and clear.

---

### Welcome

The Unofficial *Hard Lee Ever* Canon Championship is an open, non‑commercial invitation to create and publish a Canon of your choosing.

Participation is voluntary and free.

---

### What You’re Signing Up For

By signing up, you are indicating your intent to:
- Create a coherent body of work (“Canon”)
- Publish it publicly by **December 12, 2026**
- Allow it to be reviewed informally for recognition

You retain full ownership of your work.

---

### What This Is Not

- Not a competition for prizes
- Not a credential or certification
- Not a ranking system
- Not a commercial program

Recognition is symbolic and social only.

---

### Use of AI

AI tools may be used freely as assistants, collaborators, or accelerators.

There are no restrictions on tooling.

---

### Recognition Categories

Recognition may be awarded in multiple categories, including:
- Best Overall Canon
- Cross‑Domain Work
- Crowdsourced Effort
- Structural Coherence
- Creative or Innovative Use of AI

Categories may evolve based on submissions.

---

### Important Notes

- No fees or purchases required
- No rights are claimed over submissions
- Participation does not imply endorsement
- This event is unofficial and independently hosted

---

### Closing

This Championship exists to create a **temporary structure** where effort can be seen and celebrated without judgment.

When the moment passes, the structure dissolves.

What remains is the work.
