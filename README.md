## 📣 Announcement:  
### The Unofficial “Hard Lee Ever” 2026 Canon Championship  
*(Officially Means Nothing)*

**Status:** Unofficial · Non‑Commercial · Open Participation  
**Timeline:** Spring 2026 – December 2026  
**Hosted Online:** dev.umaywant2.win

---

### 🧠 What Is This?

The Unofficial *Hard Lee Ever* Canon Championship is an open, community‑driven invitation to **create your own Canon** — a coherent body of work, framework, archive, or system of thought — using modern tools, including AI assistance.

This is not a contest for prizes.  
This is not a credential.  
This officially means nothing.

It *does*, however, create a shared moment in time to build something meaningful, visible, and complete — and to recognize the effort publicly.

---

### 🛠️ What Does “Create Your Own Canon” Mean?

Participants are invited to design and publish a Canon of their choosing, which may include (but is not limited to):

- A structured framework or theory  
- A cross‑domain synthesis  
- A documented research archive  
- A community‑built or crowdsourced effort  
- A creative or technical system with internal coherence  

AI tools may be used as collaborators, assistants, or accelerators.  
Individual, group, and community submissions are all welcome.

Submissions may be hosted on GitHub or any publicly accessible platform.
