## Scoring and review process text for the sign-up page

### Evaluation method

Final reviews will be performed by **three independent AI reviewers**. Each submission will be scored using the same **weighted scoring matrix**. Scores will be combined to determine category winners.

- **Per‑AI scoring:** Each AI assigns a score from 0–10 for each rubric dimension.
- **Weighted total:** Each dimension score is multiplied by its weight.
- **Final score:** The three weighted totals are averaged.

> **Note:** AI scoring is an approximation tool for consistency and scale—not a claim of objective truth.

---

## Weighted scoring matrix

### Weights

| Dimension | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Coherence and internal consistency | 0.22 |
| Evidence and scientific ingredients | 0.18 |
| Reproducibility and auditability | 0.14 |
| Novelty and insight | 0.14 |
| Cross-domain synthesis | 0.10 |
| Documentation quality and navigation | 0.10 |
| Responsible AI use and attribution | 0.07 |
| Craft and completeness | 0.05 |

### Dimension definitions

- **Coherence and internal consistency:** Clear thesis, stable definitions, no contradictions, strong through-line.
- **Evidence and scientific ingredients:** Uses standard ingredients where applicable (claims, references, methods, assumptions, limitations).
- **Reproducibility and auditability:** Others can follow the steps, verify claims, and trace sources/decisions.
- **Novelty and insight:** Non-obvious contributions, useful reframing, original synthesis, or new structure.
- **Cross-domain synthesis:** Bridges domains without hand-waving; preserves meaning across translations.
- **Documentation quality and navigation:** Readable, well-indexed, clear entry points, good information architecture.
- **Responsible AI use and attribution:** Transparent use of AI, citations where relevant, no deceptive authorship.
- **Craft and completeness:** Feels finished; scope matches execution; minimal broken links/holes.

## Winner determination

### Overall placements

- 🥇 **Best Overall Canon**
- 🥈 **Second Overall**
- 🥉 **Third Overall**

### Category awards

Category winners are selected using either:
- **Highest score on a relevant subset of dimensions**, or
- **Highest overall score** among submissions that clearly qualify.

Suggested category mappings:
- 🌐 **Best Cross‑Domain:** Cross-domain synthesis (primary), coherence, evidence
- 🤝 **Best Crowdsourced Effort:** documentation, auditability, craft, coherence
- 💥 **Most Mind‑Blowing:** novelty (primary), coherence
- 🧩 **Best Structural Coherence:** coherence (primary), documentation
- 🚀 **Best Use of AI Assistance:** responsible AI use (primary), auditability, documentation
